Teacher Shortage Analytic Interactive Dashboard

One of my most significant projects is the analytic dashboard for the Ministry of Education and Science of Mongolia. This project involves advanced analytics on Mongolia's largest educational data system, ESIS. The primary motivation behind this project is to analyze the extent of the current teacher shortage and to predict future trends. The analytic dashboard comprises nine sub-dashboards, each meticulously modeled after five critical use cases: teacher shortage, teacher movement, teacher supply, teacher salary, and teacher demographics. This comprehensive analysis helps stakeholders understand and address the pressing issue of teacher shortages in Mongolia.
